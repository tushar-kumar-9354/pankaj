Refund Policy

Clear and transparent guidelines for refund requests on our professional consulting services.

Our Commitment to Fairness

At Anjali Bansal & Associates, we strive to provide exceptional consulting services that meet your specific needs and expectations. However, we understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise. Therefore, we have established the following refund policy to ensure clarity and fairness in all our engagements.

This policy applies to all consulting services unless otherwise specified in a signed agreement.

General Refund Policy

01

No Refunds for General Consulting Services

Due to the bespoke nature of our consulting services, which often involve customized solutions, in-depth research, and personalized advice, refunds for general consulting services are generally not provided.

Our services require significant upfront investment of time and expertise in analysis and preparation of customized solutions.

02

Exceptional Circumstances for Refunds

We may consider a partial or full refund in the following exceptional circumstances:

Significant Breach of Contract

If Anjali Bansal & Associates fails to fulfill its obligations as outlined in the signed agreement.

Termination Due to Force Majeure

If the engagement is terminated due to unforeseen circumstances beyond the control of both parties (e.g., natural disasters, pandemics).

Misrepresentation of Services

If the services provided significantly differ from the agreed-upon scope of work as outlined in the contract.

Refund Request Procedure

1

Submit Written Request

All refund requests must be submitted in writing to Anjali Bansal & Associates at anjalibansalcs@gmail.com within 10 days of the termination of services.

2

Provide Documentation

The request must clearly state the reason for the refund and include supporting documentation (if applicable).

3

Review Process

Anjali Bansal & Associates will review the request and respond within 7 to 10 business days.

For a smooth review process, please include your contract reference number and all relevant correspondence in your refund request.

Important Disclaimer

This refund policy is subject to change at any time without prior notice. Please refer to the most up-to-date version of this policy on our website.
