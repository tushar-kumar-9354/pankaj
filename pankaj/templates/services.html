Corporate Compliance & ROC Services

Ensuring regulatory adherence and statutory compliance for businesses

Our comprehensive corporate compliance services ensure your business meets all statutory requirements under the Companies Act, 2013 and other relevant regulations. We handle end-to-end compliance management to keep your business in good standing.

Company Incorporation

End-to-end incorporation services for Private Limited, Public Limited, LLP, and One Person Companies including name approval, DIN, DSC, and incorporation filing.

Annual Compliance

Comprehensive annual compliance including board meetings, annual general meetings, financial statement preparation, and annual return filing (Form MGT-7, AOC-4).

ROC Filings

Timely filing of various forms with the Registrar of Companies including change in directors, registered office, capital, and other statutory changes.

Board & Committee Support

Comprehensive board meeting support including notice drafting, agenda preparation, minutes recording, and follow-up on action items.

SEBI & Listed Company Compliance

Specialized services for listed companies and capital market regulations

Expert guidance and compliance management for companies listed on stock exchanges. We ensure adherence to SEBI regulations, LODR requirements, and other capital market norms.

SEBI LODR Compliance

Comprehensive compliance with SEBI Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements including corporate governance, disclosure norms, and periodic filings.

Corporate Governance

Implementation and monitoring of corporate governance practices including board composition, committee structures, and governance reporting.

Insider Trading Regulations

Design and implementation of code of conduct for prevention of insider trading, monitoring of trading windows, and compliance reporting.

Disclosure & Reporting

Timely disclosure of material events, financial results, shareholding patterns, and other regulatory requirements to stock exchanges.

FEMA & International Transactions

Expert guidance on cross-border transactions and foreign exchange regulations

Navigating the complexities of Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) regulations for inbound and outbound investments, external commercial borrowings, and other cross-border transactions.

Foreign Investments

Structuring and compliance for foreign direct investment (FDI), foreign portfolio investment (FPI), and investment by NRIs/PIOs under automatic and approval routes.

Overseas Investments

Advisory and compliance for overseas direct investments (ODI) by Indian companies, including joint ventures and wholly-owned subsidiaries abroad.

External Commercial Borrowings

Structuring, documentation, and compliance for ECBs, including foreign currency convertible bonds and foreign currency exchangeable bonds.

Branch/Liaison Office

Establishment and compliance for branch offices, liaison offices, and project offices of foreign companies in India.

Startup Advisory & Fundraising

Specialized services for startups and growing businesses

Comprehensive legal and secretarial support for startups from incorporation to fundraising and beyond. We help startups navigate regulatory requirements while focusing on growth.

Startup Incorporation

End-to-end startup incorporation with guidance on optimal business structure (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC) and startup India registration.

Fundraising Support

Comprehensive support for fundraising rounds including term sheet review, due diligence, shareholder agreements, and SEBI compliance for angel/VC funding.

ESOP Design & Implementation

Regulatory Compliance

Ongoing compliance management tailored for startups, including annual filings, board meetings, and investor reporting.
